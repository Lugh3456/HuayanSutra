华严经

大方广佛华严经

唐 · 实叉难陀译 · 八十卷

华严经是大乘佛教最宏博的经典，毗卢遮那佛于菩提树下成道后直示法界实相：一切万法相互含摄、重重无尽，如因陀罗网，珠珠相映。其核心旨趣是事事无碍——宇宙万象皆是佛身，一微尘中含三千大千世界。

The Avatamsaka Sutra is the grandest scripture in the Mahayana tradition. Spoken by Vairocana Buddha immediately after his enlightenment, it reveals the cosmos as Indra's Net — an infinite web of interdependent jewels, each perfectly reflecting every other. Its central teaching is 事事无碍: all phenomena interpenetrate without obstruction, and the entire universe is the body of the Buddha.
