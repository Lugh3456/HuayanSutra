Avatamsaka Sutra

大方广佛华严经

Mahayana · 大乘 · Huayan

The Avatamsaka Sutra — the Flower Garland Scripture — is the grandest of all Buddhist sutras. Spoken by Vairocana Buddha immediately after his enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree, it describes the cosmos as an infinite web of interdependence, symbolised by Indra's Net (因陀罗网): a net of jewels in which every jewel perfectly reflects every other. Its central revelation is that all phenomena interpenetrate without obstruction — 事事无碍 — and that the entire universe is the body of the Buddha.
