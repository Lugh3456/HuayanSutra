第十七品 · 初发心功德品

大方广佛华严经

← 第十六品 目录 Contents 第十八品 →

尔时，天帝释白法慧菩萨言：佛子！初发菩提心，所得功德，其量几何？法慧菩萨言：此义甚深，难说难知，难分别、难信解、难证、难行、难通达、难思惟、难度量、难趣入。虽然，我当以佛神力，为汝略说。佛子！假使有人以无量无边不可说诸珍宝物，布施一切众生……所得福德，不如初发菩提心一念功德百分之一，千分之一，百千亿那由他分之一。

逐句解释

初发菩提心，所得功德，其量几何？

天帝释提问：初发菩提心所得到的功德，有多大？这个问题承接第十六品「初发心时，便成正觉」，将视角从「境界」转向「功德」——发菩提心不只是一个境界的认识，它本身产生巨大的实际功德（merit，积极的精神力量）。法慧菩萨首先坦承：「此义甚深，难说难知」——这件事太深了，很难说清楚，也很难理解。但他还是「略说」，以比较的方式来显示。

What is the measure of the merit obtained by initially giving rise to the mind of enlightenment?

Indra's question invites Fahuishu to quantify the unquantifiable. The chapter's method is comparison: it sets up an extraordinarily large act of ordinary generosity and then asserts that even this falls infinitely short of the merit generated by a single genuine moment of bodhicitta. This is not meant to discourage generous giving — giving is praised throughout the Avatamsaka. It is meant to convey the qualitative difference between actions performed within the ordinary framework of self-interest and actions arising from the total reorientation of the self toward the liberation of all beings. Bodhicitta is not an addition to the path; it is the transformation of the person walking the path. That transformation is, by nature, immeasurable.

假使有人以无量珍宝布施一切众生……所得福德，不如初发菩提心一念功德百分之一

法慧菩萨用一系列逐步升级的比较：假设有人用无量珍宝布施一切众生，所得福德极大——但仍不及初发菩提心一念功德的百分之一。继而推进：即使有人将上述功德再无量倍地扩大……仍不及初发菩提心一念功德的千分之一、百千亿分之一……这种无穷递进的比较方式，是《华严经》的经典修辞：将有限的量一再无限放大，仍然趋近于零，来显示菩提心功德的彻底无量。

Suppose a person gave immeasurable precious treasures to all beings… the merit obtained would not equal one hundredth — not one thousandth — not one part in a hundred thousand billion — of the merit of a single moment of the initial resolve of bodhicitta.

The escalating comparisons enact mathematically what the chapter asserts philosophically: bodhicitta is incommensurable with ordinary merit because it represents a categorical shift, not a quantitative accumulation. Ordinary giving, even on an incomprehensible scale, remains within the framework of "I give, they receive" — a transaction, however large and generous. Bodhicitta dissolves this framework entirely: there is no longer a "self" generating merit for its own account; there is only the aspiration that all beings be liberated, with no remainder and no exception. This is not a bigger action; it is a different kind of being. Different in kind cannot be compared to different in degree — no amount of the latter reaches the former.

何以故？菩提心者，与一切佛功德等

为什么发菩提心的功德如此无量？因为「菩提心者，与一切佛功德等」——菩提心与一切诸佛的功德相等。发菩提心，就是在心的层面与佛陀的心相等——不是在能力或证量上相等，而是在志向与方向上等同：都是为了一切众生的解脱。「等佛」不是「已经成佛」，而是「与佛同心」。同心即是同行，同行即是同归。

Why? Because the mind of enlightenment is equal to the merit of all Buddhas.

"Equal to the merit of all Buddhas" (与一切佛功德等) is the chapter's most astonishing claim. It does not say "approaching" or "resembling" — it says "equal." The logic: bodhicitta is the aspiration to attain full Buddhahood for the benefit of all beings. That aspiration, in its purest form, is indistinguishable in its direction and orientation from what all Buddhas have already accomplished. The seed contains the tree; the intention contains the outcome. This is not a claim that the bodhisattva has already done everything a Buddha does — clearly they have not. It is a claim about the quality of the orientation: pointing in exactly the same direction as Buddhahood is, in the most important sense, to already be aligned with Buddhahood.

发菩提心者，能为无量众生作调御师……令至涅槃

菩提心不只产生功德，它还产生能力：发菩提心者能成为无量众生的「调御师」（引导者、驯导者），引导他们直至涅槃。「调御师」是佛陀十号之一，意为调御丈夫——能够引导众生调伏自心、趋向解脱的导师。发菩提心者，在志向上继承了佛陀的工作；这份志愿本身，就开始发挥着引导和救度的力量。

One who gives rise to the mind of enlightenment can become the guide and tamer of innumerable beings, leading them to nirvana.

"Guide and tamer" (调御师) is one of the ten epithets of the Buddha — it describes the Buddha's capacity to meet each being's condition and guide them skillfully toward liberation. The chapter claims that this capacity is present in seed form in every person who genuinely gives rise to bodhicitta. The aspiration to liberate all beings is not passive; it begins to organise the practitioner's energy, perception, and action around that purpose from the moment it arises. This is why the Mahayana tradition places such emphasis on bodhicitta as the starting point: not as a concept to be understood but as a living orientation that, once genuinely adopted, begins immediately to transform the person who holds it.

总结 · Summary

第十七品以无量的比较，显示初发菩提心的功德无可估量——无量珍宝布施的功德，不及菩提心一念功德的百千亿分之一。原因是：菩提心与一切佛功德等。发菩提心者，不只自身积累无量功德，还能成为无量众生的引导者，令其趋向涅槃。这一品是对「初发心时，便成正觉」的功德面的展开：菩提心在质量上与佛心等同，在方向上与解脱等同。

Chapter 17 makes the Avatamsaka's most extravagant claim about the power of bodhicitta: its merit exceeds that of any conceivable act of ordinary generosity — by infinite margins — because it is qualitatively equal to the merit of all Buddhas. No amount of giving, however vast, can reach bodhicitta by accumulation, because bodhicitta is a different kind of thing: the complete reorientation of the self toward the liberation of all beings, with no remainder, no exception, and no self-interest at the centre. The person who genuinely holds this aspiration has, in the most important sense, already joined the Buddhas' work. The rest of the path is the expression, stabilisation, and fulfilment of what the first moment already contained.
