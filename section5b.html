华藏世界品第五之二 尔时，普賢菩萨復告大众言： 「诸佛子！此无边妙华光香水海東，次有香水海，名：离垢焰藏； 出大蓮华，名：一切香摩尼王妙莊严；有世界种而住其上，名：遍 照刹旋，以菩萨行吼音为体。此中最下方，有世界，名：宮殿莊严 幢；其形四方，依一切宝莊严海住，蓮华光網雲弥覆其上，佛刹微 尘数世界围遶，纯一清净，佛号：眉间光遍照。此上过佛刹微尘数 逐句解释

此中最下方有世界名：最胜光遍照，以一切庄严为际……此上过须弥山微尘数世界，有世界名：摩尼华枝轮

华藏世界海是一个垂直的宇宙层叠：从最下层「最胜光遍照」世界，逐层向上，经过须弥山微尘数的层次，达到更高的「摩尼华枝轮」。每一层都有其形状、颜色、庄严与住持佛陀。这种垂直的宇宙结构图，是华严世界观的空间化表达：觉悟有层次，而每一层都完整。

At the very bottom is a world named Most Victorious Light All-Pervading, bounded by all adornments … above this, past world-layers equal to Mount Sumeru's dust motes, is a world named Mani-Flower Branch Wheel.

The vertical cosmology of the Flower Treasury — layer upon layer of world-systems ascending from the lowest to the highest — maps a hierarchy of enlightened environments. Each level has its form, its adornments, its buddha. This is not merely cosmological decoration; it encodes the Huayan understanding that reality has depth: every situation, every realm, every moment of experience participates in a continuum of awakened quality that runs from the lowest to the highest without a gap.

彼一一世界中，各有佛出兴，其名各异

「各有佛出兴，其名各异」——每一层世界中都有佛出现，而且各有不同的名号。这意味着觉悟并非一个孤立事件，而是在无量世界中持续发生的现象。「名各异」是重要的：佛名随应化境界不同而不同，但所证的觉悟是同一的。名称是方便，觉悟是真实。

In each of those worlds, a buddha appears, each with a different name.

'A buddha appears in each world, each with a different name' establishes one of the Avatamsaka's central claims: enlightenment is not a singular historical event but an ongoing, universal occurrence. In every world-system, in every epoch, beings attain awakening — and they teach under names appropriate to that context. The infinity of buddha-names is not a plurality of different truths but the same truth manifesting through infinite responsive forms. One dharmadhatu, infinite expressions.

此世界种，有须弥山微尘数世界，其最下方有世界名：净光照耀……

第五品的核心方法是「详说」：通过无数世界的名字、形状、颜色、庄严，让读者在阅读中体验宇宙的无量。这不是枯燥的列举，而是一种意识扩张的修行：每一个名字都是一扇门，门后是一个完整的宇宙；每个宇宙都有其佛、其众生、其庄严。法界是无穷无尽的门的集合。

This world-cluster has worlds equal to Mount Sumeru's dust motes; at its lowest level is a world named Pure Light Illumination…

The fifth chapter's method is systematic enumeration — naming world after world, colour after colour, adornment after adornment. This is a deliberate meditative practice: each name is a door, and behind each door is a complete universe with its own buddha, beings, and adornments. Reading it attentively, one is conditioned to experience the sheer scope of the dharmadhatu not as an abstract concept but as a direct cognitive encounter with genuine infinitude.

此华藏庄严世界海中，一切世界海之内，有十佛刹微尘数的世界种

华藏世界海中的世界种，在数量上达到「十佛刹微尘数」。这种具体化的无量（不是「很多」，而是「十佛刹磨成微尘之数」）是华严的语言特征：它通过把无量与具体的物理操作（磨碎佛刹）绑定在一起，让「无量」不再是一个抽象词，而是一个要求读者进行某种心理建构的动态概念。

Within this Flower Treasury Adorned World-Ocean, among all the world-oceans within, there are world-clusters equal to the dust motes of ten buddha-fields.

The number 'dust motes of ten buddha-fields' is characteristic Avatamsaka mathematics: not 'many' or 'infinite' in the abstract, but a specific unimaginable quantity derived from a concrete (if impossible) operation — grinding ten entire buddha-fields into dust-motes and counting them. This linguistic move forces the mind to engage concretely with the concept of infinity. The reader must attempt the calculation, fail, and in failing, encounter directly the limits of conceptual thought — which is precisely the point.

彼诸世界种中，有一名：普照十方炽然宝光明……其最下方有世界名：最胜光遍照

「普照十方炽然宝光明」——遍照十方的炽然宝光明，是这个世界种的名字。华严中，事物的名字往往是其本质的直接描述：名字即境界。宝光炽然、普照十方，不是装饰性的描述，而是对该世界种在法界坐标中功能的宣示。这与华严的「名实相符」原则相呼应。

Among those world-clusters, there is one named Universally Illuminating the Ten Directions Blazing Jewel-Radiance… its lowest world is named Most Victorious Light All-Pervading.

'Universally Illuminating the Ten Directions Blazing Jewel-Radiance' — Avatamsaka names are descriptions of reality, not arbitrary labels. In the Huayan view, a true name directly expresses the nature of what it names: form and name are non-dual. This naming principle applies throughout the sutra: the names of worlds, bodhisattvas, and buddhas encode the specific qualities they embody. To encounter a name attentively is to encounter the thing named.

如是诸世界种，一一各有诸世界，不相杂乱，各有所依，无非净愿之所建立

「不相杂乱，各有所依，无非净愿之所建立」——无量世界种，各各差别，互不混乱，各有其依住之处，而一切都建立于清净愿力之上。这句话完美总结了华严的宇宙伦理学：多样性（差别）、秩序（不相杂乱）与清净动力（净愿）三者共同构成法界的庄严结构。

These world-clusters each possess their own worlds, without confusion or disorder; each has its own foundation; all are established by nothing other than pure vows.

'Without confusion, each with its foundation, all established by pure vows' (不相杂乱，各有所依，无非净愿之所建立) is the Flower Treasury chapter's summary formula. Infinite diversity (countless world-clusters), perfect order (no confusion), and pure ground (vow-power) — these three together describe the dharmadhatu as Vairocana sees it: infinitely varied, perfectly coherent, and ultimately grounded in the compassionate aspiration of practice. The universe is not chaos but the structured expression of inexhaustible loving intention.

总结 · Summary

卷九深入华藏世界品的宇宙志。一层层世界，从最下层向上排列，每层都有其形状、颜色、庄严与住持佛陀；每一世界种中包含须弥山微尘数的世界；所有这些世界种共同构成华藏世界海。本卷的核心贡献是让读者体会「不相杂乱，各有所依，无非净愿之所建立」：无量差别中的秩序，混乱中的和谐，皆来自菩萨清净愿力的根基。这是华严宇宙论的伦理维度：美来自善，序来自愿。

Scroll nine continues the Flower Treasury cosmology: layer upon layer of worlds ascending vertically, each with shape, colour, adornments, and resident buddha; each world-cluster containing Mount Sumeru's dust motes worth of worlds. The chapter's guiding formula crystallises: 'without confusion, each with its foundation, all established by pure vows.' Infinite diversity, perfect order, and pure ground — three qualities that together describe the dharmadhatu as seen by Vairocana's wisdom. The Flower Treasury chapter's ethical dimension: beauty arises from goodness, order arises from aspiration.
